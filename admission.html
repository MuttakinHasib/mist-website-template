Admission Procedure

At Diploma engineering levels, Ten Years of formal education (S.S.C) Normal and Vocational in any group , Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) , having a minimum of GPA 2.5 or 2nd divisions. Students are admitted on the basis of past academic achievements, potential for academic success as reflected in the admission application. Applicant should be provide 3copies passport size Color photo and S.S.S or equivalent courses Registration card and Academic transcript.

Hostal And Accommodation Facilities

MIST is non-residential but assistance is provided to international students in finding good accommodation at reasonable cost in the vicinity of the Collage. MIST keeps up-to-date record of off-campus housing availability. MIST Housing Officer provides assistance in locating accommodation for individual or group of students. Large number of houses near the Collage are available for rent at reasonable cost and the Housing Officer undertakes negotiation with the owner, if required, on behalf of the international student. Any student requiring additional assistance or prior booking for accommodation may contact MIST Admissions Office.
