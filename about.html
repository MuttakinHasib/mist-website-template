Brief History of Model Institute of Science and Technology (MIST)

Model Institute of Science & Technology ( MIST) is a private polytechnic institute located at West Bhurulia in Gazipur. It was founded in 1998 by Professor Dr. Engr. Mohammad Abdul Mannan, for the spreading technical education to the common purposes of life. MIST campus overlooks Dhaka University of Engineering Technology (DUET) and Gazipur uppazilla parishad. MIST has slowly evolved over the years while retaining a focus on the scientific and technological roots upon which it was founded. Over the last Nine teen years, MIST has grown into 11th department in diploma in engineering namely (i) Computer Engineering (ii) Electrical Engineering (iii) Electronics Engineering (iv) Civil Engineering (v) Architecture Engineering (vi) Textile Engineering (vii) Garments Design and Pattern Making Engineering (viii) Mechanical Engineering (ix) Telecommunication Engineering (x) Automobile Engineering

Goal of Model Institute of Science and Technology (MIST)

The Faculty of Engineering provides its graduates with top quality education in engineering and, preparing them to practice their professions competently to meet the ever-changing needs of society, and to continue learning their discipline, allowing them to move into other related fields. Excellence and diversity in research are essential to our mission and build on our strong collaboration with industry and government research laboratories. The Faculty encourages a spirit of entrepreneurship amongst its students and personnel. The Faculty strives to provide a learning environment that promotes excellence and innovation, ethical practice and responsibility towards society. The Faculty is committed to a culture built on respect of the individual and fair treatment for all. Our mission is to advance the well-being of society through excellence in teaching, research and service that exploits the rapidly changing technical diversity of engineering. We achieve this within a collaborative environment that stimulates faculty, staff and students to reach their highest potential through life-long learning

Scholarship and STEP project of MIST

At present there is a provision of offering stipends to 40% of the enrolled students of the institute @2000/= per semester. The stipends of the students other then first semester are awarded on the basis of there results in the final examinations ot the preceding semesters. Marit good conduct satisfactory progress and regular attendance in the corresponding semester are considered as criteria for enlistment for award of stipend. 10% of the total number of stipends as result for female students. In case of eligible female students are not being available, the stipends are awarded to eligible male students. Student’s failing in any subjects in their final examination of the preceding semester are not entitled to stipends for the semester. First Semester students are awarded stipends on the basis ot the result of their S.S.C of Equivalent examinations.

Cultural Program of MIST

Gone are the days when only academic programs were the only activities organised in colleges and all students focused on that. With the changing trend we have noticed a major difference in the field of academics too. Students don’t want to be bookworms; they look for other options that could help them in the future. Besides, this in this high tech world, every student wants to acquire knowledge in every field to stay updated. Co-circular activities play a vital role in the life of students, read on to know how. Extracurricular activities increase opportunities for social interaction and new relationship development. As most of these activities are group-oriented which have students from different niches, which gives them a chance to more know about people of different passions and cultures. Besides, this they can also find clubs or groups that share similar social, religious or even entertainment interest for socialization. Interaction with people of different backgrounds helps in development of interpersonal skills of students. Extracurricular activities teach students how to work for a common goal and this ultimately develops a sense of responsibility in them. It increases the level of confidence and also teaches them how to co-operate and work with people in different conditions. They learn to face the challenges that come in education and career. Extracurricular activities like sports require a person to remain physically fit. Thus students have to be particular about their health and diet. They have to take diet which can make them physically stronger and also do exercise daily. They start loving their body, which is very important for a happier life. Most of the employers love to hire people who are all rounded. Sustained involvement in more than one activity reflects the talent and potential of the student. Hiring officials look for these talents in people. Besides, this when applying for admission in colleges for higher degrees, these students get a preference too. While pursuing these activities in college along with education students learn prioritization and time management skills too. These academically and co-circularly talented students have well-groomed personality, which helps them to face the world in a better way.
